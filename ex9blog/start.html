Garlic

Garlic is the small, white, round bulb of a plant that is related to the onion plant. Garlic has a very strong smell and taste and is used in cooking. COLLOCATIONS: clove of ~ We use garlic to add a very strong flavor to the food. If you don't want a strong taste of garlic, add one clove instead of two.
